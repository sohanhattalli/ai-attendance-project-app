The Teacher's Journey

Step 01

Secure Login

Sign in with your teacher account and jump straight into your classroom tools.

Step 02

Command Center

Create subjects, run photo-based FaceID attendance, and review past sessions from one dashboard — including attendance records and roster stats.

Step 03

Share & Enroll

Each course gets a join link and QR code. Students scan or tap to enroll — no manual roster entry.

Step 04

Voice Roll-Call

Record classroom audio and let AI match each “present” to enrolled students who have a voice profile — ideal when a group photo isn't available.
